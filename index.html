The history of Kalida

It all began with...

Koert imagined the ancient Greeks playing tic-tac-toe on a marble column, and enjoyed the novelty of playing on a board with wrap-around-edges. Thus, Kalida (from the Greek words for "beautiful form(s)") was born.

Welcome to
Kalida
A GAME OF 5-IN-A-ROW STRATEGY
5-in-a-row
the most basic way to win
Place five of your markers in a row, column, or diagonal.
BOUNCE
BOUNCING OFF THE WALLS
Diagonal lines can "bounce" off board edges to form winning patterns. Up to two bounces are allowed per winning line.
DIAGONAL WRAP
A real brain twister
Diagonal lines can “wrap around” the board - left connects to right, top connects to bottom. Up to two wraps allowed per winning line.
